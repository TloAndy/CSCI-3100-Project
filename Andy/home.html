Our Goals

The “E”s in the name of our application stand for “Expectation”, “Education” and “Evaluation”. “Expectation” means that we want the public to have a clear, overall expectation of the course before the course registration. “Education” emphasizes our product is able to enhance both the teaching quality of instructors and the learning efficiency of students. “Evaluation” shows our aim of motivating students who registered the course to grade and give comments which is visible to the public so that instructors can know what can be improved.
